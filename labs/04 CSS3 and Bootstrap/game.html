Space Escape

Space Escape is a text adventure game where Per Finn, the protagonist, dares to venture the uncharted realms of planet Mars. Be prepared for thrill and suspension!

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System after Mercury. In English, Mars carries a name of the Roman god of war, and is often referred to as the "Red Planet"[15][16] because the reddish iron oxide prevalent on its surface gives it a reddish appearance that is distinctive among the astronomical bodies visible to the naked eye.

Setup

Per Finn and his assistant have succesfully landed in Mars. As soon as the engines are turned off, there is a loud knock at the door of the outer airlock. Who could that be?
